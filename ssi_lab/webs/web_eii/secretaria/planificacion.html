  • End-of-Degree Project(+)

    The end-of-degree project is a personal project to be carried out by the students in order to obtain their Degree in Software Engineering. It is to be carried out during the fourth year. As registration is still not open, there are aspects yet to be decided. However, you can already consult the Regulations on the End-of-Degree Project Subject at the University of Oviedo.

    For further information, click here.

  • Placements(+)

    Work placements are a mandatory activity within the Degree in Software Engineering syllabus. It is to be carried out during the fourth year. You can consult the Placements Regulations of the University of Oviedo.
